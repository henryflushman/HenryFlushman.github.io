VTOL Drone

Jan 2024 - Feb 2026 • Designer • Drone, Mechanical

Prototype Vertical Take-Off and Landing capable drone.

Project details

Overview

Design and production of a VTOL drone that switches between quadcopter and fixed-wing modes. Take-off and landing are handled vertically to broaden their location possibilities, once airborne it transitions to plane mode for a more efficient cruise, higher speeds and extended range. The first test flight is scheduled for december 2025.

Key features / highlights

  • 3m Wingspan
  • 500g Payload
  • 4.1kg total weight

Tech & tools

  • PTC Creo
  • 3D Printing
  • Carbon fiber
  • Pixhawk
  • Composite Design

My role

Over the course of two years, I contributed to the design and development of the tail, nacelles, fuselage, assembly tools and much more for the VTOL drone.
